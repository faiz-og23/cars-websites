About Us

The company’s founder, Ferruccio Lamborghini, was a very successful businessman who made tractors out of old military parts after World War II.


Lamborghini is an world-famous Italian company that builds some of the fastest, loudest, and most expensive cars on Earth. While they are known for luxury today, the brand actually started because of a famous argument over a tractor.
